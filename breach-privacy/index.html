PRIVACY POLICY · Driftlight Games

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Breach ("the app", package com.driftlight.breach), published by Driftlight Games, handles information. Breach is a fictional hacking-simulation game; all targets, organizations and characters in it are entirely made up. The game is designed to be played without creating an account.

Information we collect

The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number or location, and we do not run our own servers that store your personal data. Your progress (credits, gems, reputation, rig upgrades, contracts) is stored locally on your device.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — shows ads and may use your device's advertising identifier and similar data for ad delivery, frequency capping and fraud prevention. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services / Billing — processes in-app purchases and basic functionality.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases. Payments are handled entirely by Google Play; we never see or store your card details.

Children

Breach is a general-audience simulation game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings, and you can clear all local game data by uninstalling the app.
