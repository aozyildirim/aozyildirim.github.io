🗼 Sandy Stack — Privacy Policy

Driftlight Games

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sandy Stack ("the app", package com.driftlight.sandy_stack), published by Driftlight Games, handles information. We keep things simple: the game is designed to be played without creating an account.

Information we collect

The app itself does not ask for your name, email, phone number or location, and we do not run our own servers that store your personal data. Your game progress (scores, coins, gems, unlocked items) is stored locally on your device.

Third-party services

To show ads, offer purchases and keep the app working, we use trusted third-party services that may collect limited data in line with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — shows ads and may use your device's advertising identifier and similar data for ad delivery, frequency capping and fraud prevention. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services / Billing — processes in-app purchases and basic app functionality.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases. Payments are handled entirely by Google Play; we never see or store your card details.

Children

Sandy Stack is a general-audience casual game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings, and you can clear all local game data by uninstalling the app.
