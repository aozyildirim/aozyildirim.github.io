PRIVACY POLICY · KARTALINA GAMES

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Noir: Detective Files ("the app", package com.kartalinagames.noir), published by Kartalina Games, handles information. Noir is a 1920s detective mystery game where you examine crime scenes, collect clues, interrogate suspects and name the killer. The game is designed to be played without creating an account.

Information we collect

The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number or location, and we do not run our own servers that store your personal data. The detective name you optionally enter and your progress (solved cases, collected clues, reputation, gems) are stored locally on your device.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — shows ads and may use your device's advertising identifier and similar data for ad delivery, frequency capping and fraud prevention. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services / Billing — processes in-app purchases and basic functionality.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases (gem packs, a season pass and an ad-removal option). Payments are handled entirely by Google Play; we never see or store your card details.

Children

Noir is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings, and you can clear all local game data by uninstalling the app.
